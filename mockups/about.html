Site Information

About This Site

Our Mission

This site exists to make images from the lands of the Bible available free to the general public. Text accompanies images for identification and brief commentary on significance — not exhaustive coverage.

A Growing Collection

This is a growing site. Approximately 10–15 images are added each week. Coverage spans Israel, Turkey, Greece, Italy, Malta, Jordan, and Lebanon.

Dr. Carl Rasmussen

Dr. Carl Rasmussen is Professor Emeritus at Bethel College, Minnesota, and former Dean at the Institute of Holy Land Studies in Jerusalem. He holds a PhD in Ancient Near Eastern Studies and is the author of the Zondervan Atlas of the Bible.

Jesse Gavin — Web Design

Jesse Gavin (Bethel College, 2001) is a Senior Software Developer at Dow Jones. He designed and maintains this site on a volunteer basis.

Image Format

Images are provided as JPEG files in variable sizes and quality. Upgrades are planned on an ongoing basis.

Contribute Images

We welcome contributed digital images. Photographer credit will be given; no compensation is offered. Contact us to discuss.
