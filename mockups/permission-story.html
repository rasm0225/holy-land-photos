Site Information

Permission to Use

These images are offered at no cost for personal, non-commercial use (with exceptions noted below). The site requests attribution using language such as: "Image(s) courtesy of www.HolyLandPhotos.org" in written or verbal form.

Commercial Usage

Individuals interested in commercial applications should contact Dr. Rasmussen at HolyLandPhotos@gmail.com. High-resolution, non-watermarked versions are available for a fee.

Web Usage

Website publishers may use up to 4 images with proper attribution to www.HolyLandPhotos.org. Using 5 or more images requires express permission from the site. Text reproduction requires specific authorization from the content provider.

Register for Updates

Visitors can subscribe for occasional notifications about new images. The site assures subscribers they won't receive excessive emails and confirms the list won't be shared with other parties.

Additional Images

Those wishing to suggest images for the collection should contact Dr. Rasmussen via email.
