Recommended Reading

Archaeology & Geography

Archaeology and the Old Testament — Alfred Hoerth (Baker, 1998). Organized chronologically with illustrations.

Archaeology and the New Testament — John McRay (Baker, 1991). Spans Israel, Turkey, Greece, and Italy.

Zondervan Atlas of the Bible — Carl Rasmussen (Zondervan, 2010). Geographical introduction with historical-biblical commentary, maps, and gazetteer.

Guide Books

The Holy Land: An Oxford Archaeological Guide — Jerome Murphy-O'Connor (Oxford, 2008). Simply the best — and inexpensive.

Turkey: A Traveller's Historical and Architectural Guide — McDonagh.

Greece: A Traveller's Guide — Barber & Flower.

Ancient Life, History & Culture

The Ancient City: Life in Classical Athens & Rome — Connolly & Dodge. Richly illustrated with diagrams and cultural background.

Life of Paul

St. Paul — The Traveler and Roman Citizen — William Ramsay (Kregel, 2001). Covers Paul's complete journeys with updated photographs.

Video Resources

Zondervan has released a 13-lesson video series filmed in the Holy Land. An 18-minute introduction to the ancient Middle East is available on YouTube, along with a 6-minute Jerusalem overview.
