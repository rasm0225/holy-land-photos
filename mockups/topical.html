Topical Easy Find

For your convenience on this page I have assembled some suggestions for images related to a variety of topics. For example, if you are studying the life of Solomon I have assembled images that are related to that topic that you might want to check. As always, you are welcome to use our images in your personal PowerPoint presentations—if you could give credit (and/or a link) to www.HolyLandPhotos.org that would be appreciated.

Disclaimer — I may have missed a few so please also use the "Search Window" on this site. If you have any suggestions please shoot me an email.

Topics: History of Israel; New Testament: Recommended Readings; Travel.

History of Israel

History of Israel — I taught this course for many years to University Students and approached the topic chronologically—moving from the earliest to later periods. I have begun to assemble recommended images for each of the historical periods that I covered. So here goes.

Early Bronze Age (EBA, 3150–2200 B.C.) Completed 25 August 2013.

  • Arad — 19 images of EB houses, city wall, towers, temple, water system, etc.
  • Ai (et-Tel) — 3 images of EB wall, temple.
  • Megiddo — 9 images of EB temples, altar.
  • Egypt — Pyramids

Intermediate Bronze Age (IBA, 2250–2000 B.C.; possibly the time of Abraham) Completed 25 August 2013.

  • IBA settlements in the Negev (southern Israel; 5 images)
  • IBA shaft tombs (north of Jerusalem at Dhahr Mirzbaneh [Ein Samiya]; 8 images)
  • Dolmens (Golan Heights northeast of the Sea of Galilee; 3 images)

Middle Bronze Age II (MB II, 2000–1500 B.C.; the time of the Patriarchs of Genesis 12–50) Completed 26 August 2013.

  • Shechem — 10 images of the massive city wall, gates, Balage Balogh diagrams
  • Gezer — images of MB II gate city wall, and standing stones/high place
  • Dan — 5 images of the wonderfully intact MB II city gate
  • Hebron — 25 images of the Cave of Machpelah
  • Jerusalem
    • Pool Tower (above the Gihon Spring; 9 images)
    • Water Channel II (near the Gihon Spring; 9 images)
  • Ashqelon — 8 images of the MB II Gate and Glacis
  • Jericho — 2 images of the MB II Revetment Wall
  • Megiddo — 1 image of MB II/LB City Gate
  • Shiloh — 4 images of the MB II City Wall

Solomon (ca. 970–930 B.C.) (Under development 27 August 2013)

  • City Gates and Walls at Hazor, Megiddo, Gezer
  • Gezer Calendar
  • Fortresses in the Negev — Arad Citadel, Haluqim
  • Jerusalem — Temple Mount; el-Aqsa Mosque (site of Solomonic Palace?)
  • Parallels to the Solomonic Temple — Ain Dara in Syria; Cedars of Lebanon
  • Parallel to Solomonic Palace — the bit hilanni Palace at Alalakh in Turkey
  • Geshur — home town of Solomon's brother Absalom
  • Beth Shemesh — 6 images of a huge cistern

New Testament

Places Mentioned in the Gospels:

Nazareth (39 images in 3 folders), Bethlehem (32 images), Bethany Beyond the Jordan (Jordanian; Israeli), Cana of Galilee, Pools of Bethesda (in Jerusalem), Capernaum, Chorazin (site; synagogue), Bethsaida, Magdala, Mount of Beatitudes, Loaves and Fish Church, Church of the Primacy of Peter, Gergesa (Kursi), Tiberias (city; gate and theater; from above), Sychar, Mt. Gerizim, Gadara (city, theater, churches, artifacts, area), Caesarea Philippi, Mount of Transfiguration (Tabor), Jericho, Mt. of Olives (views; Dominus Flevit; Mary Magdalene Church; Gethsemane, cave of Gethsemane), Mosque of Ascension.

Paul's First Missionary Journey (Acts 13:1–14:28)

Antioch on the Orontes, Seleucia Pieria, Paphos, Perga (city, stadium, theater, bath), Pisidian Antioch (artifacts), Iconium, Lystra, Derbe, Attalia. (Completed 3 September 2013)

Paul's Second Missionary Journey (Acts 15:36–18:22)

Antioch on the Orontes, Seleucia Pieria, Derbe, Lystra, Iconium, Troas, Samothrace, Neapolis, Philippi (Lydia Baptismal [Thyatira]; South Philippi, North Philippi), Amphipolis (more to come), Thessaloniki (Forum, City, St. Demetrius Church, Monastery of Vlatadon), Berea, Athens (100+ images), Corinth (50+ images), Cenchrea, Ephesus Site, Ephesus Artifacts, Caesarea (100+ images), Antioch On the Orontes. (completed 9 October 2013).

Paul's Third Missionary Journey (Acts 18:23–21:14)

Antioch on the Orontes, Seleucia Pieria, Ephesus Site, Ephesus Artifacts, South Philippi, North Philippi, Troas, Assos Citadel, Temple, Harbor, Assos Agora, Necropolis, Theater, Miletus, Patara, Patara Lighthouse, Caesarea (100+ images). There are other places, such as Corinth, Thessalonica, Berea, etc., that Paul probably visited on this journey. See a map in a good Atlas to get an idea of the route of this Third Journey. (completed 17 October 2013).

The Seven Churches of Revelation (Revelation 1–3)

Ephesus Site, Ephesus Artifacts, Ephesus Paul/Thecla Cave; Smyrna, Smyrna Artifacts; Pergamum Acropolis, Pergamum Asclepion, Pergamum Artifacts, Pergamum Red Basilica; Thyatira; Philadelphia; Sardis; Laodicea.
